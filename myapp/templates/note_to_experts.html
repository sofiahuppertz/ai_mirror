* NOTE TO AI EXPERTS:

Throughout history, writers, filmmakers and artists, imagined society’s upcoming challenges, helping illustrate the future in ways a dry scientific report would struggle to communicate.

As a creative my goal is to encourage the public to freely start to explore ideas around AI. Consequently in this book I will avoid bogging down the reader with excessive technical descriptions, terminologies and metrics.

To further simplify the discourse I will heretically and freely use AI synonymously with AGI… Let's not be overly sensitive!

AI DEFINITIONS:

AI: Narrow AI is the type of artificial intelligence that can only perform specific tasks and lacks the ability to adapt or learn new types of things.

AGI: Artificial General Intelligence, is referred to a type of AI that performs a wide range of tasks and abilities, much like a human.

Super Intelligence: is referred to an AGI that surpasses our intelligence, becomes autonomous, and can improve its own code and objectives.
