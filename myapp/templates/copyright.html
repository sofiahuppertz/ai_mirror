This is a transcription of my notes and ideas scribbled over a decade imagining our inevitable future clash with AI.

Technology is currently advancing so fast that writing this book was a race against time. I would have liked to have sharpened the texts, but preferred to immediately release a minimum viable product that could be improved with your user feedback.

Consider this book an exercise in creative thinking. Flip through it one page at a time or read it in one go, as a sci-fi thriller for humanity’s future. Make it your captain’s log for the AI storm.
