SEBASTIAN ERRAZURIZ

Internationally acclaimed as one of the most prolific designers of his generation, Errazuriz is an expert in the art and science of creativity. His first-principles approach to the arts has resulted in a successful scientific method of innovation across creative industries.

Obsessed for the last decade with the upcoming impact of artificial general intelligence; his artworks, texts, and talks on AGI provide a framework to help society imagine and prepare for the upcoming technological revolution.

A guest speaker at Harvard’s Tech Lab, The Silicon Valley Computer Museum, The Tate Modern and New York’s Museum of Art and Design, amongst others. His creations have been showcased in more than 100 exhibitions and included in permanent museums collections around the world. Sebastian was born in Chile. He lives and works in New York.
